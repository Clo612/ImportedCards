How to Grade Pokémon Cards for Maximum Value

Learn the best practices for grading your Pokémon cards to ensure they sell for the highest possible price.

What Is Grading?

Grading is the process of evaluating the condition of a card using a 10-point scale, with 10 being "Gem Mint." The higher the grade, the more valuable the card.

How to Grade Your Cards

Use these guidelines:

  • Check the edges for wear.
  • Examine the corners for any damage.
  • Look for surface scratches and print lines.

Popular Grading Companies

Some of the top grading companies include:

  • PSA (Professional Sports Authenticator)
  • CGC (Certified Guaranty Company)
